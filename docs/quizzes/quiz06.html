Exercise 1

Let \(\mathbf{y} = \left[ {\begin{array}{cc} y_1 \\ y_2 \end{array} } \right]\), \(\boldsymbol{\theta} = \left[ {\begin{array}{cc} 4 \\ 8 \end{array} } \right]\), \(\Sigma = \left[ {\begin{array}{cc} 1 & .2 \\ .2 & 1.3\\ \end{array} } \right]\).

If \(\mathbf{y} | \boldsymbol{\theta}, \Sigma \sim MVN(\boldsymbol{\theta}, \Sigma)\), then \(y_1 \sim N(a, b)\). What is \(a\) and \(b\)?
